Plastic bags are the worst thing that humans could have made. They are used in a huge wuantity because they are cheap, easily available and convenient to use. However it is choking the environment and killing wildlife and nature. Drains are getting choked, animals are consuming plastic bags found on the roads and dying, aquatic life is getting degraded due to improper disposal of plastic bags. My prime objective is to cut the use of plastic bags and replace them with cloth bags and jute bags. Everyone have unuseful cloth and jute material at home and don't dispose them properly. I urge everyone to donate the unuseful material to us so that we can put it to use.
